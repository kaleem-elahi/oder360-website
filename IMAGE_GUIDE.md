## Image Specifications

### Recommended Sizes
- **Hero Background**: 1920x1080px (16:9)
- **Portfolio Images**: 1200x800px (3:2) or 1200x900px (4:3)
- **Featured Works**: 1200x800px (3:2)
- **Gallery Images**: 1200x900px (4:3) or 1200x800px (3:2)

### Image Formats
- **Primary Format**: JPEG (best for photos)
- **Quality**: 85-90% compression for web
- **Next.js will automatically**: Convert to WebP/AVIF, optimize, and lazy load

## Getting Images from Canva

To extract images from your Canva site:
1. Visit your Canva portfolio: https://abdul-rasheed.my.canva.site/
2. Right-click on images and "Save Image As"
3. Rename and organize according to the structure above
4. Place in the appropriate `/public/images/` directories
